## Key design decisions
- `blacklisted_accounts` only contains `tip_payment_program_id` (the program ID)
- Tip PDAs (TIP_ACCOUNT_0 through TIP_ACCOUNT_7) are NOT in the blacklist
- Config PDA is NOT in the blacklist
- Blacklist check operates on `view.account_keys()` after ALT resolution in all paths
- The same blacklist set is used for both BundleStage and BankingStage
